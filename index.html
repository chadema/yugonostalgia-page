Yugonostalgia

Yugonostalgia(Jugonostalgija) is a Klondike Solitaire game with a nostalgic theme of former Yugoslavia. The classic card symbols are replaced with crests of football clubs from the region. The game also features comments from famous sports commentators, creating a unique nostalgic experience.Yugoslavia(Jugoslavija) was the homeland of people in the following republics: Serbia(Srbija), Croatia(Hrvatska), Slovenia(Slovenija), Bosnia and Herzegovina(Bosna i Hercegovina), Macedonia(Makedonija) and Montenegro(Crna Gora).It was a land of happy people in the mountainous Balkans(Balkan).
